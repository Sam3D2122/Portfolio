Umbricius Ludus

An ambitious project developed by Italia 3D Academy and the Poliziani High School of Montepulciano. I was responsible for training the students and for the Unreal Engine development of the title. The project features several mini-games, all playable with both mouse and keyboard as well as with a controller. Additionally, using photogrammetry-based models, I handled optimization by leveraging both Nanite and Lumen technologies.

End Ink

A personal project I have been working on for a few months. It is a storytelling experience where the player embodies a drop of ink tasked with writing the word "The End" at the conclusion of a story.

Megalo Splash

A reimagining of the classic Hangman game for VR headsets. Set aboard a pirate ship, players must guess the correct word — if they lose, they fall into the water and are eaten by a shark. For this project, I was responsible for gameplay programming and optimization to ensure the best performance on VR headsets (specifically Meta Quest 2/3).

Vortic Ball

A demo currently in development that supports and utilizes SpatialLabs platform technologies. Through Acer, I had the opportunity to create this demo for their monitors and laptops that feature the mentioned technology. During development, I had the chance to work directly with some of the developers involved in building the platform's APIs.
